Drivers for ZTE Open

Works on all Windows versions

1.- Download the .zip file from here (direct download) or here (Mega).

2.- Unzip the .zip and execute the only file inside the folder (you need admin permissions).

3.- Choose your language.

4.- Now just click "Next" in all the windows and wait until the wizard finish.

5.- If all gone right, now plug your phone to the PC and let Windows do his work.

5.- Now you can see your device connected in the PC and you can begin to flash your Firefox device.

6.- Optionally , if you have installed ADB in the computer, you can try to find your device with “adb devices”. Remember activate the debug mode in the phone.
